US President Donald Trump has declared the end of the Memorandum of Understanding that suspended the US-Israeli war with Iran. The comments came after the US said Iran struck several oil tankers in the Strait of Hormuz, and the US military responded with airstrikes. Trump left the possibility of more negotiations open, but told reporters, “They can talk, but I think they’re wasting their time.”
